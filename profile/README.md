# Lead Friendly

Lead Friendly is the consolidated AI sales platform we wished existed.

Most teams running an outbound motion end up paying for a CRM, a voice-AI subscription, and a telephony account separately — three bills, three integrations, three places a contact record can drift. Lead Friendly bundles them: a full CRM (contacts, pipeline, calendar, recordings, transcripts, AI summaries), AI voice agents, telephony, browser softphone, and platform-level TCPA + FCC AI-voice compliance, on one plan.

Built for compliance from day one. Every outbound channel goes through a hard compliance gate (KYC, AUP, quarantine, E911, AI-window, velocity, PEWC, DNC obligations) before a single byte leaves the platform. STOP/HELP, CAN-SPAM postal address + one-click unsubscribe, two-party recording rules — all wired in.
